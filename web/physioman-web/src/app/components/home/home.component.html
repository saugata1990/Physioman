How to Book Online

It is assumed that you have already signed up on Physioman via the mobile app or through this website. Login to your profile using your registered mobile number and the password you have set. To book a physio, fill out the form describing your current ailment and choose payment mode(cash or online). Note that discounts and other offers are available only to our customers who pay online. Once the request is lodged, you will get a confirmation as well as an OTP on your mobile no. through SMS, and also in your email inbox. Keep that OTP secure and do not share with anyone. Only share it with our representative when requested.

Our representative will get in touch with you at the earliest either through phone or by visiting your home. The representative will decide your treatment plan and discuss the same with you. We will then assign a physiotherapist to you based on your needs and your treatment plan. Your sessions will start shortly thereafter.

Before each session, your physio will send you an OTP for the session which you must share with your physio once he/she is there for the session, for the session to start. After the session is over, you can log into your account and share your feedback on the session. Your feedback will help us know how we are doing as a service, and what are the potential areas of improvement.
