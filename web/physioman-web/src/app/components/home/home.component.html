Our Services

Physiotherapy

Medical Equipment

Our Physiotherapy services comprise a range of services including Pain Management, Neuro Rehab, Pulmonary Rehab and Geriatrics Rehab. Physioman's trained physiotherapists, who are among the best in the business, provide you with the best possible treatment at the comfort of your home.

Pain Management Pain may be caused by chronic illnesses like arthritis or temporary conditions like a sprain or a broken bone or after a surgery. The best non-drug way to deal with it is with physiotherapy. Manage chronic pain like knee pain, back pain or arthritis and other kinds of pain like a frozen shoulder with Physioman's physiotherapy. We have trained and experienced physiotherapists who help you manage pain at home.
Neuro Rehab Patients suffering from conditions like cerebral palsy, multiple sclerosis, Parkinson's disease, muscular dystrophy, diabetic and peripheral neuropathy, motor neuron disease, GB syndrome or a slipped disc often experience neurological malfunction. Our aim is to improve functionality through physiotherapy for those suffering from neurological disorders or neurological impairment caused by trauma. Our physiotherapists are trained to help improve mobility.
Pulmonary Rehab For those with pulmonary disorders, Physioman offers pulmonary rehab. Our chest physiotherapy or CPT is targeted towards mobilising or loosening secretions in the lungs and the respiratory tract. Our pulmonary rehab consists of external mechanical maneuvers, such as chest percussion, postural drainage, vibration to help with mobilization and clearance of airway secretions, diaphragmatic breathing with pursed lips, coughing and controlled coughing.
Geriatric Rehab Physioman's geriatric rehab service is physical therapy for elderly people suffering from conditions beginnig from Alzheimer's to urinary incontinence. Physiotherapy in the elderly can help improve strength, mobility, coordination, flexibility and endurance. It can also lessen the intensity of pain in those who suffer from chronic conditions like arthritis. It is especially helpful for those temporarily paralysed after a stroke.
